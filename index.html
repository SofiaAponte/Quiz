Hello there, wanderer...

Thanks for visiting my site! I currently attend school at Texas State University

My name is Sofia, and I grew up with five brothers who did not show me much in the ways of femininity, but did inspire creativity and my love for artistry. Everyone has an understanding of what they feel to be art, and what they feel to be a great song, for one thing. I love to paint- it is my outlet and my happy place. But music is something I could never live without. No matter what you are feeling, hearing the right words can change things in an instant.
